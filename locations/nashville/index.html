Why Nashville?

  • Nashville is a very collaborative community and a lot of smart engineers.
  • The Nashville Entrepreneur Center have a corner on the MBA/suits, but the overlap between “startup biz-types” and “engineers” is pretty small.
  • Tennessee is pretty business friendly, all things considered.

What does this group look like?

Right now (as of January 2024) it’s super informal. That means we hang out in a private chat room on an already-existing Slack group that many are already on (which does mean we are also subject to their CoC). We have ad-hoc get-togethers coordinated by Brian.

As we determine the level of interest, we can develop more structure.
